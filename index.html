 Welcome to NutriValue where we value a healthier lifestyle. Our goal is to inspire people to eat healthier and reduce food waste. You will find recipes that are convenient to make based on the ingredients available in your kitchen. The recipes provided will have nutrition values to ensure you get a balanced diet. If you’re missing ingredients, we will locate you to the nearest grocery store. Get started today!
How it works

Step 1: Add your ingredients!


Step 2: Select the type of cuisine!


Step 3: Select any dietary restrictions!


Step 4: Press find recipes!


Step 5: Choose your recipes!


Step 6: Still missing ingredients? Type in your location on our map to locate a grocery nearby!


Step 7: Enjoy your home cooked meal!
